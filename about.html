About Us

Who We Are

Curly Tales is a hair salon specializing in many services to suit your curly hair needs. Curly Tales is located in the heart of Washington, DC and was founded in 2002 by stylist Lisa Hill. She sought to teach women how to care for their unique curly textures with the appropriate tools, techniques and products. The salon has four senior hair specialists who are trained in the latest techniques and receive regular training from top specialists in the field.
